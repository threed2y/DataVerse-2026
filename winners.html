🥇 1ST PLACE  ·  ENTROPY  ·  HARNITYA NAROLA & MEET KARANGIYA

Stopping the Bleed: From Blanket Discounts to Uplift Modeling

A prescriptive ML dashboard tackling Mercato's ₹148M promotion paradox. By deploying a CATE T-Learner Uplift Model and XGBoost churn predictor, the solution identifies the 28% of customers promotions actually work on — recovering ₹65M in destroyed margin without losing a single transaction. Includes a live budget simulator that lets you reallocate spend and watch the P&L recover in real time.
