Competition Structure

COMPETITION

Two rounds. Three days. One winner.

Round 01

Data Visualization

📅 26th February, 2026
Qualifying Round

Participants demonstrate data storytelling and visualization skills using real datasets. Choose one domain and craft insights that communicate impact.

  • Participants select one domain dataset from the available options
  • A data dictionary and guiding questions are provided at the start
  • Use any visualization tool or programming language of your choice
Duration3 Hours
QualifiersTop 3 / Domain
ToolsAny

SUBMIT

Visualization dashboard or notebook + short insight summary paragraph explaining key findings.

Round 02

DataVerse Finals

📅 27–28th February, 2026
Grand Finale

Finalist teams tackle real-world scenario-based problem statements in a 9-hour intensive datathon. Mentors available for guidance throughout.

  • The Safe Lending — Lending Club Risk Analysis
  • The Promotional Paradox — Mercato Sales Behaviour
  • The Urban Heat Mystery — Navapur Climate Analysis
Duration9 Hours
Presentations28 Feb
Results28 Feb Eve

SUBMIT

Solution approach document + models/analysis + final presentation, notebook, or web application.

Judging

Evaluation Criteria

ROUND 1 — DATA VISUALIZATION

  • ◆Clarity and relevance of visualizations
  • ◆Quality of insights and storytelling
  • ◆Accuracy and appropriate use of data
  • ◆Creativity and effective design choices
  • ◆Overall coherence and interpretability

ROUND 2 — FINALS

  • ◆Problem understanding
  • ◆Technical correctness
  • ◆Innovation
  • ◆Impact and feasibility
  • ◆Quality of presentation
